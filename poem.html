Here’s a Poem Just for You ❤️

HUM DO KE DO SAAL

We fought, misunderstandings came our way,

We stood firm, together, through challenging days.

Two years have passed, and love still glows,

In every challenge, we cherished brighter days.


From taking care of me like a little child,

Handling my mood swings, to going wild.

Your patience and love are beyond measure,

In your arms, I find my true treasure.


In these two years only few meetings were made,

But those memories are so precious and never gonna fade.

But many more to come that's my dream,

Marriage, kids, a peaceful life ahead.


With you by my side, I have no fear,

Your love heals me, year after year.

Two years today, and many more to come,

Our journey together has only just begun.
